Comprehensive Payroll Solutions

Payroll Processing

Complete monthly salary computation with accuracy and efficiency. We handle everything from basic salary calculations to complex variable pay structures.

What We Handle

  • Monthly Salary Computation: Accurate calculation of monthly salaries including all earning components such as basic pay, HRA, allowances, special allowances, and other fixed components as per your salary structure.
  • Variable Pay, Incentives & Arrears: Processing of performance-based incentives, sales commissions, quarterly/annual bonuses, and arrears from salary revisions with proper tax treatment.
  • Overtime, Shift Allowances, Bonus & Leave Encashment: Computation of overtime pay as per labor laws, shift allowances for different work schedules, festival bonuses, and leave encashment for earned/privilege leaves.
  • Full & Final Settlements: Complete FNF processing including recovery of notice period shortfall, leave encashment, gratuity calculation, bonus pro-rata, loan/advance recoveries, and exit formalities within 3 working days.

Key Benefits

  • 100% accurate salary calculations reviewed by senior team
  • Fast turnaround time - payroll completed in 2-3 days
  • Automated bank transfer file generation in multiple formats
  • Digital payslips with self-service portal access
  • Variance analysis and exception reporting
  • Complete audit trail for all calculations
  • Seamless integration with attendance and leave systems

Deliverables

  • Individual payslips (digital PDF format)
  • Consolidated payroll register

Compliance Management

100% statutory compliance with zero penalties. We ensure all your payroll compliances are filed accurately and on time.

Statutory Compliance Coverage

  • PF (Provident Fund) Computations & Filings: Monthly PF calculations for both employer and employee contributions, ECR filing on the unified portal, preparation of Form 12A, Form 3A, and handling PF withdrawals, transfers, and advance claims.
  • ESIC (Employee State Insurance) Computations & Filings: ESIC contribution calculations, monthly challan generation, online filing on ESIC portal, IP registration for new employees, and assistance with medical benefit claims.
  • Professional Tax (PT) Computations & Filings: State-wise PT calculations based on applicable slabs, monthly/half-yearly/annual PT returns, challan generation, and PT registration for new locations.
  • Labour Welfare Fund (LWF) Computations & Filings: LWF deductions as per state regulations, timely payment of challans, annual compliance filing, and registration support.
  • TDS on Salaries (Section 192): Monthly TDS calculations based on income projections, quarterly TDS return filing (Form 24Q), preparation of Form 16 for all employees, TDS payment challans, and rectification support for mismatches.
  • Form 16 & TDS Returns: Annual Form 16 Part A and Part B generation, consolidated TDS return filing, employee-wise tax computation sheets, investment declaration management, and support during employee ITR filing.
  • Statutory Registers & Challans: Maintenance of Form T, wage registers, attendance registers, leave records, overtime registers, and all other registers required under various labor laws.

Compliance Calendar

  • Proactive reminders for all statutory due dates
  • Monthly compliance checklist tracking
  • Audit-ready documentation maintenance
  • Liaison with PF/ESIC/PT offices when required
  • Regular updates on changes in labor laws

Deliverables

  • Monthly PF and ESIC challans with payment proof
  • Quarterly TDS returns (Form 24Q) with acknowledgments
  • Annual Form 16 for all employees
  • PT challans and returns for applicable states
  • Statutory registers in prescribed formats
  • Compliance status dashboard
  • Filing acknowledgments and payment receipts

Comprehensive Database Management

  • Leave & Attendance Integration: Seamless integration with biometric devices, RFID systems, or mobile-based attendance apps. Automated synchronization of attendance data, leave balance management, LOP calculation, shift roster management, overtime tracking, and holiday calendar maintenance.

  • Employee Master Maintenance: Continuous updates of employee data for address changes, bank account modifications, marital status updates, dependent information, nomination details, and emergency contact information. Data accuracy audits and regular cleanup of dormant records.
